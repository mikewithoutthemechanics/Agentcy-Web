# Daily Blog Generator for Agentcy Website
# Run: openclaw cron add --schedule "0 9 * * *" --task "Generate a new blog post for Agentcy website. Read projects/agency-web/src/components/Blog.tsx, write a new post about SMEs adopting AI, business automation, or software development trends. Add it to the posts array, commit, build, and deploy to Vercel."

## Topics to rotate through:
- SME AI adoption stories
- South African business tech trends
- Automation case studies (anonymised)
- Software development best practices
- AI search optimisation (GEO/AEO)
- Integration patterns and architecture
- Industry-specific AI applications (retail, health, logistics, finance)
- Digital transformation for small businesses
- WhatsApp automation for SA
- Cost-benefit analyses of tech investments
